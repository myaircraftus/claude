# Spec 1 — Frontend Overhaul for myaircraft.us

## Mission
Redesign the customer-facing front end so it feels: modern, premium, cleaner, less text-heavy, more visual, easier to understand, less confusing, more trustworthy, more aviation-specific, more "real product."

## Brand Positioning
- Modern aircraft records and maintenance intelligence platform
- Built for aircraft owners, mechanics, operators, brokers, buyers, sellers, and maintenance teams
- Focused on searchable aircraft logbooks, digitized records, intelligent ingestion, organized archives, and cleaner workflows
- Practical, trusted, aviation-native, and highly usable
- Premium without looking corporate or boring

## Core Pricing Rules
- $100 per aircraft / month
- $100 per mechanic / month
- $1,000 per aircraft logbooks (onsite scanning)
- Scanning can be paid at $100/month per aircraft
- Customers with existing scans: upload directly, no scanning fee
- Account setup: FREE
- Ingestion assistance: FREE
- Accuracy support: FREE

- Colors: white / off-white / deep navy / slate / subtle metallic gray, optional blue accent

- Cards: 16–24px radius, subtle shadow, strong hover states
- Spacing: increased whitespace, every section breathes
- Buttons: primary solid, secondary outline, tertiary text, sticky mobile CTA
