# Mechanic Portal 2.0

Our mechanic and shop customers asked for three things: better work order management, visibility into revenue, and a parts catalog they could actually search. All three shipped this week.

## Work orders

Every work order now includes a checklist, labor tracking, and a customer-facing PDF that generates automatically. When a work order is completed, it rolls forward into an invoice with one click.

## Revenue analytics

New dashboard showing billed hours, labor vs. parts mix, and job profitability by aircraft type. Filters by date range, technician, and customer.

## Parts catalog

We imported 100,000+ aviation parts with cross-references, superseded-by relationships, and pricing from our marketplace partners. Search by part number, description, or aircraft applicability.

## What's next

In Q2 we ship estimates-to-invoices, customer statements, and a mobile companion for technicians in the hangar. Let us know what else you need.
